To build with the Lynx API, you need a sandbox account provisioned by Nayax. Submit the integration request form and your sandbox environment will be ready within 1-2 business days.


## Submit the request form

Go to [lynx-integration-request.nayax.com](https://lynx-integration-request.nayax.com) and complete the two-step form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in your integration details">
    Enter the following information:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Country** | Your country of operation |
    | **First Name** | Your first name |
    | **Last Name** | Your last name |
    | **Email Address** | The email address for your developer account |
    | **Business Name** | Your company or organization name |
    | **Operator ID in Nayax Core** | Your numeric Operator ID |

    <Accordion title="How to find your Operator ID in Nayax Core">
      1. Open Nayax Core and go to **Operator** in the left sidebar.
      2. Select an entry from the list and click **Search**.
      3. Your ID is listed under **Company Info** as **Operator Id**.

      If you don't have access to Nayax Core, contact your Nayax case manager or sales representative.
    </Accordion>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check the box to confirm you agree to the Privacy Policy.
    Go back to the email field, click **Send Code**. A one-time verification code is sent to the email address you provided. 
    Enter the code to verify your email and proceed to the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and sign the terms and conditions">
    Read the General Terms and Conditions for Professional Services (v. 1.05), then complete the signature section:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Business Name** | Your company or organization name |
    | **Title** | Your job title |
    | **First Name and Last Name** | Your full name |
    | **Email Address** | The email address you verified in the previous step |
    | **Digital signature** | Your drawn or typed signature |

    Click **Sign and Submit** to complete the request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect after submitting

Nayax processes your request automatically. Within 1-2 business days you will receive a welcome email containing:

- Integration instructions for the Lynx API
- Access credentials for the Nayax Core sandbox environment
- Links to the sandbox Lynx platform

Nayax provisions your sandbox account with a standalone hierarchy and full Lynx platform permissions.
