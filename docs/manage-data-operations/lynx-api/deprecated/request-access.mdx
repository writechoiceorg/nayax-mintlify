title: "Create Sandbox Account"
description: "Submit the Lynx integration request form to receive your sandbox credentials and start building."
---

To build with the Lynx API, you need a sandbox account provisioned by Nayax. Submit the integration request form and your sandbox environment will be provisioned automatically.

Before you begin, locate your **Operator ID in Nayax Core**. Open Nayax Core, go to the **Operator** menu in the left sidebar, and check the **Company** section. Your Operator ID is listed there. If you cannot access Nayax Core, contact your Nayax case manager or sales representative.

## Submit the request form

Go to [lynx-integration-request.nayax.com](https://lynx-integration-request.nayax.com) and complete the two-step form.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill in your integration details">
    Enter the following information:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Country** | Your country of operation |
    | **First Name** | Your first name |
    | **Last Name** | Your last name |
    | **Email Address** | The email address for your developer account |
    | **Business Name** | Your company or organization name |
    | **Operator ID in Nayax Core** | Your numeric Operator ID |

    Check the box to confirm you agree to the Privacy Policy, then click **Send Code**. A one-time verification code is sent to the email address you provided. Enter the code to verify your email and proceed to the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and sign the terms and conditions">
    Read the General Terms and Conditions for Professional Services (v. 1.05), then complete the signature section:

    - Business Name
    - Title
    - First Name and Last Name
    - Email Address
    - Digital signature

    Click **Sign and Submit** to complete the request.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What to expect after submitting

Nayax processes your request automatically. After submitting, you will receive a welcome email containing:

- Integration instructions for the Lynx API
- Access credentials for the Nayax Core sandbox environment
- Links to the sandbox Lynx platform

Nayax provisions your sandbox account with a standalone hierarchy and full Lynx platform permissions.
