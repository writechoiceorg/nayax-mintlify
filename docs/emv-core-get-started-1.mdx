The Nayax OEM POS Solution provides a pre-certified EMV module that simplifies the development of card-present kiosks. By integrating the EMV Core, you can focus on your applicationâ€™s UI and business logic while we handle the complexities of transaction processing.

<Note>
**Version Compatibility**

This documentation applies to EMV Core v1.4, which is the current version.
</Note>

## Identify Your Device

After installation, you must [Obtain Your Device ID](https://developerhub.nayax.com/docs/emv-core-device-id). This unique identifier is required to communicate with the Nayax gateway, initialize the EMV Core, and begin processing test transactions.
