FIS Cortina lets you configure how the system handles edge cases, such as when the fiscal provider does not respond in time.

## Timeout Handling

The FIS Cortina API manages timeouts differently depending on the transaction type and state. A timeout occurs when the fiscal provider does not respond within the configured time period.

FIS Cortina offers two configuration options for timeout handling:

- **Ignore and Proceed:** The service ignores the issue and treats the case as a successful registration.
- **Cancel the Transaction:** When a timeout occurs before the product is dispensed, the transaction is canceled and the product is not dispensed.

<Warning>
**Timeout Result**

Although the timeout may originate externally (Tax Authority or Fiscal Integrator), the Nayax Engine always records a Timeout status.
</Warning>
