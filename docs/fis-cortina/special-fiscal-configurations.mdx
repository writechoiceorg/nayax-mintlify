## Timeout Handling

The FIS Cortina API manages timeouts differently depending on the transaction type and state. A timeout occurs when no response is received from the fiscal provider within the configured time period.

There are two main configuration options for dealing with a timeout:

- **Ignore and Proceed** — The service ignores the issue and treats the case as a successful registration.
- **Cancel the Transaction** — When a timeout occurs before the product is dispensed, the transaction is canceled and the product is not dispensed.

<Warning>
**Timeout Result**

Although the timeout may originate externally (Tax Authority or Fiscal Integrator), the status recorded in the Nayax Engine is always a Timeout.
</Warning>
