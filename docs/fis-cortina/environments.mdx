FisCortina has two environments. URLs are not publicly published and are shared directly by your Integration Engineer.

| Environment | Purpose | Access |
|---|---|---|
| Sandbox | Development and integration testing | URL provided by your Integration Engineer at kickoff |
| Production | Live fiscal registration | Provisioned after certification approval |

Both environments require HTTPS. URLs use port `443` (default) or `8443`.

## Device Requirements

To use FisCortina, your Nayax device must meet the following minimum firmware versions:

| Component | Minimum Version | Release Date |
|---|---|---|
| MAIN | 4.8.24.1-RC02 | Dec 12, 2023 |
| POS | 2.4.9.8-rc01 | — |

Contact your Nayax Sales representative to procure a device with the required hardware and firmware.
