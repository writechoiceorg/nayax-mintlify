FisCortina supports card, cash, and offline fiscal registration. Card transactions are enabled by default. Cash and offline registration are optional and require your Integration Engineer to enable them.

## Card Transactions

FisCortina supports three card payment flows:

| Flow | Description | Register Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| [Pre-Selection](/docs/fis-cortina/glossary) (1-step) | Product price is known at card presentation. Single sale call. | After Sale is approved |
| [Pre-Selection](/docs/fis-cortina/glossary) (2-step) | Separate authorization and settlement calls. | After Settlement is confirmed |
| [Pre-Authorization](/docs/fis-cortina/glossary) | Initial authorization holds a default amount; settled once the final amount is known. | After Settlement with final amount |

## Cash Transactions

Cash transaction registration is **optional and disabled by default**. When enabled, a `Register` call is sent after a confirmed cash sale.

<Note>
**Enabling cash registration.** Contact your Integration Engineer to enable cash transaction fiscal registration. Availability is subject to regional regulatory requirements.
</Note>

Cash transactions (`PaymentMethodId=3`) send a reduced field set compared to card transactions. These are confirmed production behaviors:

- `DeviceInfo.FWVersion` and `DeviceInfo.Type` are not sent
- `MachineInfo.Name`, `ExternalId`, `Type`, `City`, and `ZipCode` are not sent
- `MachineInfo.Country` and `GeoLocation` are sent as empty objects `{}`
- `Products.CatalogProductId` may be sent as an empty string

## Offline Transactions

Offline transaction registration (EMV/contactless approved without an online authorization) is available for specific regions only. It is **disabled by default**. Contact your Integration Engineer to enable it.

## FiscalCommandType Values

The `BasicInfo.FiscalCommandType` field in each request indicates the type of fiscal operation:

| Value | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `0` | PREAUTHORIZATION | Pre-authorization hold. Supported but not in active use. |
| `1` | SETTLEMENT | Settlement of a pre-authorized or 2-step pre-selection transaction. |
| `2` | CANCEL | Cancellation or void of a transaction. |
| `3` | REFUND | Refund. Not yet supported. |
| `4` | PRESELECTION | Single-step sale. Most common card flow. |
| `8` | CASH | Cash transaction registration. |
