FisCortina supports card and cash fiscal registration. Both are enabled by default. To disable cash registration, contact your Integration Engineer.

## Card Transactions

FisCortina supports two card payment flows:

| Flow | Description | Register Trigger |
|---|---|---|
| [Pre-Selection](/docs/fis-cortina/glossary) (1-step) | Product price is known at card presentation. Single sale call. | After Sale approval |
| [Pre-Selection](/docs/fis-cortina/glossary) (2-step) | Separate authorization and settlement calls. | After Settlement confirmation |

<Note>
  **Pre-Authorization is not supported.** FisCortina does not support the Pre-Authorization flow (initial hold settled once the final amount is known).
</Note>

## Cash Transactions

FisCortina supports cash transaction registration, enabled by default. Cash transactions send a reduced field set compared to card transactions: device and machine details are omitted or sent as empty objects. See [Cash Registration](/docs/fis-cortina/register-cash) for the full field reference.
