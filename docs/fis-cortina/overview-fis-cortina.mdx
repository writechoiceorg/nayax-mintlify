FIS FisCortina is an API fiscal platform and service designed to handle the crucial fiscalization processes. This includes communicating transactions to third-party Fiscal providers, who forward the data to the relevant local tax authorities.

Fiscalization laws vary by country. Compliance requirements can range from online reporting before a product is dispensed to batch reporting at the end of the day. FIS FisCortina abstracts this complexity for you.
