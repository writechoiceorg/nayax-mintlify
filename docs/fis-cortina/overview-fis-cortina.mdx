FIS FisCortina is an API fiscal platform and service designed to handle the crucial fiscalization processes. This includes communicating transactions to third-party Fiscal providers who later forward the data to the relevant local tax authorities.

Because fiscalization laws vary by country, compliance requirements can range from online reporting before a product is dispensed to resending data for failed fiscal transactions and batch reporting at the end of the day. FIS FisCortina abstracts this complexity for you.
