Fiscalization is the process of reporting transactions to tax authorities. Requirements vary widely by country, ranging from online reports before a product is dispensed to batch reports at the end of the day. FIS Cortina handles this fiscal flow through its API.

The fiscal flow is composed of two essential transactions: **Register** and **Void**.
