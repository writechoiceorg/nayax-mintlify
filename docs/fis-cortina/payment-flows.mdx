Fiscalization is the process of reporting transactions to tax authorities. Requirements vary widely by country, potentially requiring online reports before product dispensing or batch reports at the end of the day. FIS Cortina handles this fiscal flow through its API.

The fiscal flow is composed of two essential transactions: **Register** and **Void**.
