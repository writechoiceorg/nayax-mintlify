---
title: "Google Pay Integration Setup"
---

To enable Google Pay as a payment method with the Nayax eCom SDK, follow these steps to complete the integration:

1. Contact your Nayax Solution Architect with the URL from where Google Pay transactions will originate (your merchant domain).

2. Wait for confirmation. Your Nayax Solution Architect will configure the necessary adjustments on the Nayax side and notify you when the setup is complete.
