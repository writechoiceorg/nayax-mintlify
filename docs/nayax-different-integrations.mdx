Nayax provides three versatile integration methods tailored to meet various operational needs, allowing seamless integration with its ecosystem:

<Columns cols={3}>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="Lynx"
  href="/docs/lynx-overview"
>
  Connect to the Nayax Core management platform and manage your machines, devices, inventory, and prepaid cards from your own environment.
</Card>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="Marshall"
  href="/docs/marshall-sdk"
>
  Connect any unattended machine with Nayax's payment devices (VPOS Touch, Onyx), utilizing physical connectivity.
</Card>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="Cortina"
  href="/docs/overview-cortina"
>
  If your organization has its own customer app, loyalty card, or closed-loop payment method, you can easily accept it on any Nayax POS with a quick Cortina integration.
</Card>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="Amazon SQS Setup"
  href="/docs/overview-1"
>
  Retrieve real-time information on your sales and operations, including transactions, machine telemetry, and alerts via Amazon SQS services.
</Card>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="EMV Core"
  href="/docs/overview-2"
>
  Connect any unattended machine with embedded Nayax payment devices (Uno line).
</Card>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="TweezerComm"
  href="/docs/overview-tweezer"
>
  Integrate Nayax's attended payment device (Nova line) with your Point of Sale, utilizing physical connectivity.
</Card>
<Card
  icon="link"
  title="Spark"
  href="/docs/spark"
>
  Remotely integrate your platform with Nayax devices (server-to-server integration). Ideal for POS and platform integrations.
</Card>
</Columns>
